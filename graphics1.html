Week 1 : Graphics Design

Lesson Overview :

This lesson introduces the fundamental concepts of graphics design. You'll learn about the role of graphics design in communication, the importance of visual elements, and the basic tools and software used in the industry.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the definition and significance of graphics design.
  • Identify key visual elements (color, typography, imagery).
  • Familiarize yourself with design software (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator).

Lesson Content


1. Introduction to Graphics Design

Graphics design is a creative discipline that combines art and technology to communicate ideas through visual content. It plays a crucial role in branding, advertising, and digital media.



2. Key Visual Elements

Understanding visual elements is essential for effective design. These elements include:


  • Color: The psychology of color and its impact on design.

  • Typography: The art of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing.

  • Imagery: The use of images, illustrations, and icons to convey messages.


3. Design Software Overview

Familiarizing yourself with industry-standard software is vital. Tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator are essential for creating stunning visuals.
